The European Travel Compromise

Every day, 55,000 people cross EU borders, and 75% are ready for sustainable options. But today's travel forces an impossible choice. We're told to pick one: speed, sustainability, or convenience. Why can't we have all three?

The Speed Trap

We're offered a false choice: fly at a blistering 839 km/h, or crawl on a conventional train at 150 km/h. While 70% of people prefer trains, the speed is simply not competitive for international travel, forcing a compromise before the journey even begins.
